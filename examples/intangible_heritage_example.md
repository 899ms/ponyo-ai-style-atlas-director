# Example: 非遗视觉短片

## 用户输入
我要做一个非遗主题短片，有神性，但不要宗教说教，要高级、有东方美学。

## 推荐风格组合
敦煌壁画风 35% + 电影写实风 30% + 东方玄幻风 20% + 厚涂插画风 15%

## 六宫格逻辑
1. 矿物颜料在粗粝壁面上被晨光照亮
2. 细小颜料颗粒沿着壁画纹路流动
3. 飘带和云纹从二维壁面轻轻展开
4. 主体从壁画中半立体浮现
5. 颜色注入纹路，形成神性高潮
6. 收束成一张东方非遗主视觉

## 视频提示词
15-second cinematic cultural heritage short film, 16:9. Camera begins with an extreme macro shot of mineral pigment grains on a weathered mural wall, warm grazing light revealing cracks and handmade texture. The camera slowly tracks along ancient painted lines; blue-green and ochre pigments softly awaken inside the pattern. Flowing ribbons and cloud motifs emerge from the wall like low-relief sculpture, not magic particles. The camera gently pushes through the mural surface into a poetic Eastern visual world, ending on a majestic heritage hero image with mineral colors, fresco texture, and calm sacred atmosphere. No text, no logo, no random glow, no cheap fantasy CGI.
